Building local, social networks for neighbours in Europe.

We lower the hurdle to meet the people living around you and help neighbours to connect in real life. This creates social and sustainable communities at local level.

We, Berlin-based Good Hood GmbH, develop and operate hyperlocal, social neighbourhood networks in Europe. nebenan.de is Germany's largest social network for neighbours and mesvoisins.fr is market leader in France.

Strong and lively neighborhoods work on a small scale and for each individual. They improve our quality of life. We believe this new kind of social network would strenghten social cohesion within our local communities and hold the key to solve a lot of the challenges our modern democratic societies are facing today, such as demographic change, loneliness, anonymisation of society, sustainable use of resources, inclusion and integration.

No algorithms, no filter bubbles, no sold data. We want to help revive neighbourhoods with an easy-to-use digital tool and commitment.
